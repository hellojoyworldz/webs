Company History CTK stands for Cosmetics Technics Korea. A leading innovator in formulation and cosmetic packaging with more than 280 patents and offices in North America, Europe, China and Taiwan. CTK works for large multinational partners in the cosmetics industry including Chanel, Dior, Givenchy, Loreal, MAC, NARS, NYX, Kat Von D, Sephora, Marc Jacobs, Smashbox, Zara, and others. CTK Bio is the newest addition to the CTK Group. ​ Over the last 20 years, CTK has discovered advanced technologies and formulas, while collaborating with cosmetic R&D centers, university labs, and design labs to provide the fi nest products to our partners. Not only that, through industry-academic cooperation, CTK is steadily expanding the scope in all industries, primarily beauty by contributing papers to famous scientific journals such as ACS (American Chemical Society). Since the foundation of Cosmetics Technics Korea 2001 (KOSDAQ: CTK Co Ltd), the CTK Group continues to expand its services and business across the globe with the establishment of CTK Cosmetics, CTK Brand Lab, CTK E-Vision, CTK Investment, CTK Fulfillment, CTK Bio. CTK Cosmetics continues to grow as the world’s best beauty full-service company that enables beauty brands to complete the process of branding to design, content creation, product planning and development, production and logistics in just one step.
